About

As avid readers as well as talkers, Hank and Giorgia found themselves in need of a space to share their thoughts of their most recent reads, prompted by a surge of bookish ferver brought on by each other's presence and their love for literature. However, there seems to be no public forum with a desirable interface and a pleasing aesthetic that they both searched for. Thus, being CS students, they made their own. Thus, BookWorms was born out of the ecstatic need to not only consume more good literature, but to spread what we have read outwards for the world to see, as well as reflect on the subject matter that passes from page to mind.
